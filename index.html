Mandir Guidelines & Prashadi

Mandir Rules (Dos & Don'ts)

  • No Leather Items: Strictly ignore leather items. Do not bring leather belts, wallets, or purses inside the mandir.
  • Simple Clothes: Wear loose, comfortable clothes. Avoid heavy dressing up or fancy outfits. Keep it simple and spiritual.
  • Normal Footwear: Wear normal slippers/chappals as you have to leave them at the shoe stands outside.
  • No Mobiles/Cameras: Electronics are prohibited. Free lockers are available outside to store them.

Satvik Prashadi Details

  • Where to get: Buy authentic Dwarkadhish Prashadi only from the official mandir trust counters located inside the mandir premises near the exit gates.
  • Types of Prashad: You will get dry Satvik Bhog (like Magaj/Besan Ladoo, Mishri) which remains fresh for many days so you can easily take it home for relatives.
  • Timings: The prashad counters are open during all normal Darshan timings.

Dhwaja Arohan (Flag Hoisting)

The holy flag on the mandir शिखर (peak) is changed 5 times a day by devotees.

2nd: ~ 9:00 AM
3rd: ~ 11:30 AM
4th: ~ 5:00 PM
